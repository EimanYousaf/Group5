What Manipulated You?

1. Urgency / Scarcity

Countdown timers and “limited-time bonus” messages were used to pressure fast decisions instead of careful thinking.

2. Social Proof / Authority

The fake leaderboard and AI recommendation messages suggested that “top players” or an intelligent system knew the best choice.

3. Misdirection

A booster was pre-enabled, the accept option was visually dominant, and the decline option used confirmshaming language.

Why This Was Included

This game demonstrates how interface design can shape behavior through subtle pressure, framing, and unequal visibility of choices.
