Debrief

What Was Manipulative?

1. Urgency / Scarcity

The timer and bonus warnings pressured you to act quickly instead of carefully.

2. Social Proof / Authority

The leaderboard and system recommendations suggested that “top players” and the AI knew best.

3. Misdirection

The booster was pre-enabled, the accept button was much stronger visually, and the decline option was harder to notice.

Purpose of the Assignment

This game shows how interface design can influence behavior even when the experience looks polished and professional.
